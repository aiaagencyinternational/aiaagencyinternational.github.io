Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AIA Studios ("we", "us"), operated by AIA Agency International, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our content scheduling and publishing service.

1. Information we collect

  • Account authorization data: when you connect a social account (e.g. via TikTok Login), we receive access and refresh tokens and a basic account identifier needed to publish on your behalf.
  • Content you schedule: the videos, images, captions and metadata you choose to publish through the service.
  • Performance data: basic public metrics of your own posts (such as views, reach, likes) used to provide analytics back to you.

2. How we use information

  • To publish and schedule content to your own connected accounts at your request.
  • To provide you with performance analytics of your own content.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your data for advertising to third parties.

3. Data from TikTok

When you authorize the TikTok integration, we access only the scopes you grant (such as publishing video and reading basic profile and post information). This data is used solely to provide the service to you and is handled in accordance with TikTok's Developer Terms and Policies.

4. Storage & security

Access tokens are stored in restricted, access-controlled application storage and are used only to perform actions you request. We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service.

5. Revocation & deletion

You can disconnect your account at any time via the platform's app settings (e.g. TikTok → Security & Permissions → Apps) or by contacting us. Upon revocation or request, we delete the associated tokens and data. To request deletion, email luciahot0919@gmail.com.

6. Third parties

The service interacts with the platforms you connect (e.g. TikTok, Meta) via their official APIs. Your use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.

8. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: luciahot0919@gmail.com.
