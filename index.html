Frequently asked questions

The short version of how AIA Studios treats you and your content.

Does AIA Studios post to my account?

Yes — but only to your own account, only content you queued, and only after you explicitly authorized AIA Studios via the platform's official login (e.g. TikTok Login Kit). We never post anywhere else.

How do I revoke access?

Anytime, in two ways: disconnect inside AIA Studios, or remove the app in your platform's settings (e.g. TikTok → Settings → Security → Apps). Revocation takes effect immediately.

What data do you store?

Only what's needed to run your queue: your access token (stored in restricted application storage), your queued media and captions, and per-post performance metrics. Details in our Privacy Policy.

What about AI-generated content?

If your content is AI-generated, AIA Studios applies the platform's required disclosure automatically — for TikTok that's the built-in AIGC label on every published post.
