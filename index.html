Social content management for creators.

AIA Studios is a scheduling and publishing tool that helps creators and their teams plan, schedule and publish short-form video and images across their own connected social accounts — reliably and on time.

What we do

Creators connect their own social accounts and authorize AIA Studios to publish content on their behalf. We handle scheduling, queue management, posting at optimal times, and performance analytics — so creators can focus on making content instead of manual uploading.

TikTok integration

With the account owner's explicit authorization, AIA Studios uses the TikTok Content Posting API to upload and publish videos to the owner's own TikTok account. We access only the data needed to publish content and report basic performance. Access can be revoked by the account owner at any time. AI-generated content is labeled accordingly.
